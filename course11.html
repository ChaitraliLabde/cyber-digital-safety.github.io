📊 Real Case Studies of Cyber Attacks

1️⃣ Yahoo Data Breach (2013–2014)

🔹 Incident:

Over 3 billion user accounts were compromised, including emails, passwords, and security questions.

🔹 Impact:

Massive identity theft risk and reputational damage.

🔹 Lesson:

Use strong, unique passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and regularly update security measures.

2️⃣ WannaCry Ransomware Attack (2017)

🔹 Incident:

A ransomware virus infected hundreds of thousands of computers across 150 countries, encrypting files and demanding ransom.

🔹 Impact:

Hospitals, businesses, and government agencies were heavily affected.

🔹 Lesson:

Keep systems and antivirus updated, avoid suspicious downloads, and backup important data regularly.

3️⃣ Cambridge Analytica Facebook Scandal (2018)

🔹 Incident:

Personal data of 87 million Facebook users was harvested without consent for political profiling.

🔹 Impact:

Raised global awareness about data privacy and unethical use of social media data.

🔹 Lesson:

Be cautious about the permissions you grant apps and review privacy settings frequently.

4️⃣ Indian ATM Malware Attack (2018)

🔹 Incident:

Criminals installed malware on ATMs across India, stealing millions of rupees in a coordinated scheme.

🔹 Impact:

Financial losses and disruption of banking services.

🔹 Lesson:

Avoid using unsecured ATMs, monitor bank statements regularly, and report suspicious activity immediately.

5️⃣ NotPetya Cyber Attack (2017)

🔹 Incident:

A global malware attack primarily targeting Ukraine spread worldwide, disrupting major companies and government services.

🔹 Impact:

Millions in damages due to lost data and halted operations.

🔹 Lesson:

Regular backups, software updates, and network segmentation can reduce impact.
