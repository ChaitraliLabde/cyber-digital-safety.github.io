Cyberbullying is harassment, threats, or humiliation via digital platforms.

üîπ Signs of Cyberbullying:
  • Frequent upset or anxiety after using devices
  • Avoiding school or social interactions
  • Changes in behavior or mood
  • Receiving threatening or abusive messages
üîπ How to Respond:
  • Do not respond or retaliate
  • Save evidence (screenshots, messages)
  • Report the abuse to the platform and, if necessary, to school authorities or parents
  • Talk to a trusted adult about the experience
üîπ Example:

A child receives mean messages on social media. Reporting the account and blocking the bully prevents further harassment.
