⚖️ Policy & Laws

Comprehensive guide to cybersecurity policies, regulations, and legal frameworks worldwide

🏆 Top Policy & Law Instruments

🇮🇳 India 2023

Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

India's primary personal-data law enacted Aug 11, 2023. Sets consent, processing, breach-notification and compliance obligations; implementing rules being released.

Why it matters: Major change for how Indian organisations process personal data.
🇪🇺 EU 2018

GDPR - General Data Protection Regulation

The EU's landmark data-protection regulation establishing data subject rights, strict consent rules, cross-border transfer limits and substantial fines.

Why it matters: Global standard that influences many national laws.
🇪🇺 EU 2023+

NIS2 Directive

Updated EU directive (replaces NIS) raising cybersecurity requirements, incident reporting and supervision across critical sectors.

Why it matters: Expands scope of organisations that must implement mandatory security measures and report incidents.
🇺🇸 USA 2021

Executive Order 14028

Improving the Nation's Cybersecurity - US federal EO that drives secure software/supply-chain measures, zero-trust adoption and federal agency actions.

Why it matters: Major US policy shaping software supply chain and federal cybersecurity practices.
🌍 International 2001

Budapest Convention on Cybercrime

Council of Europe Convention - the main multilateral treaty for international co-operation on computer crime and harmonising offences.

Why it matters: Primary international instrument to enable cross-border law enforcement cooperation.

🌐 International & Multilateral Instruments

🌍 Global 2001

Budapest Convention on Cybercrime (Council of Europe)

Harmonises cybercrime offences; fosters international co-operation and mutual legal assistance.

🌍 Global Ongoing

UN, INTERPOL & Europol Cybercrime Policy & Advisories

Cross-border law enforcement coordination, advisories and strategic guidance. (Summaries and alerts published by the agencies.)

🇪🇺 European Union

🇪🇺 EU 2018

GDPR (2018)

EU data protection regulation; strong user rights and strict cross-border transfer rules.

🇪🇺 EU 2023+

NIS Directive → NIS2 (2023+)

Network & information security rules, incident reporting, national CSIRTs cooperation.

🇪🇺 EU 2019

EU Cybersecurity Act

ENISA mandate + certification framework - strengthens ENISA and creates an EU-wide cybersecurity certification framework.

🇪🇺 EU 2022

Digital Services Act (DSA) & Digital Markets Act (DMA)

Platform responsibilities, transparency and gatekeeper rules (affect misinformation, content moderation & platform obligations).

🇪🇺 EU Proposed

Cyber Resilience Act (CRA)

Proposed regulation to increase security of connected products across lifecycle (device/software).

🇺🇸 United States

🇺🇸 USA 2021

Executive Order 14028 (2021)

Federal programmatic requirements to improve cybersecurity, software supply chain security and incident response.

🇺🇸 USA Ongoing

CISA Guidance & Incident Reporting Programs

Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency - central federal cybersecurity coordination.

🇺🇸 USA Various

Sectoral Laws & Standards

HIPAA (health), GLBA (financial), FERPA (education) and industry guidance (SEC cyber rules for public companies, PCI-DSS for payment cards). US approach is largely sectoral.

🇮🇳 India

🇮🇳 India 2000

Information Technology Act, 2000 (IT Act)

Original Indian e-commerce & cybercrime law; defines offences and liability for intermediaries (amended over time).

🇮🇳 India 2023

Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

India's statutory data protection framework (enacted Aug 11, 2023) - rules/notifications being phased in.

🇮🇳 India Recent

MeitY Draft Rules & IT Rules

Intermediary Guidelines & Digital Media Ethics Code - rules for intermediaries, social media regulation, and newer drafts addressing AI/deepfakes.

🇮🇳 India Ongoing

CERT-In Directives & Advisories

Operational incident reporting obligations, vulnerability handling, and sectoral advisories (e.g., incident reporting timelines, audit guidance).

🌏 Other Key Jurisdictions

🇦🇺 Australia Recent

Cybersecurity Act

Critical Infrastructure reforms, mandatory reporting rules - governments increasingly imposing mandatory reporting and security obligations.

🇨🇳 China 2017-2021

Comprehensive Data Laws

Cybersecurity Law (2017) + Data Security Law (2021) + Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL, 2021) - comprehensive state-centric data controls, cross-border data rules.

🇸🇬 Singapore Recent

PDPA & Cybersecurity Act

Personal Data Protection Act and Cybersecurity Act - strengthened regulatory controls.

🇯🇵 Japan Ongoing

Personal Information Protection

Act on the Protection of Personal Information and cybersecurity strategies.

🔧 Standards, Frameworks & Guidance

🛡️ Standard 2014

NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)

US NIST framework widely adopted internationally for risk management.

📋 Standard 2013

ISO/IEC 27001 & 27002

International standards for information security management systems (ISMS).

🎯 Framework Ongoing

CIS Controls

Prioritized cybersecurity controls used by many organisations.

💳 Standard 2004

PCI-DSS

Payment card industry data security standard (for card data).
