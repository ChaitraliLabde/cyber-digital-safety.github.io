Report Cyber Crime

How to report

  1. Stay calm and collect evidence: screenshots, URLs, emails, phone numbers.
  2. Do not delete chats or messages; note date, time, and handles.
  3. File a complaint on the National Cyber Crime Portal.
  4. For financial fraud, contact your bank immediately and then report.

About Us

👥

1️⃣ Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of cybersecurity enthusiasts, educators, and developers committed to making the digital world safer for everyone. Our mission is to empower users with the knowledge and tools to navigate the internet securely.

🎯

2️⃣ What We Do

Our platform offers interactive courses, real-life case studies, quizzes, tutorials, and resources covering topics like online threats, password security, social media awareness, and data privacy. We provide actionable tips to help users stay protected in today's digital environment.

💡

3️⃣ Why We Matter

As the internet becomes an integral part of our lives, cyber threats such as identity theft, phishing, and online fraud are increasing. We aim to bridge the knowledge gap and equip users—students, parents, and professionals alike—with the skills to safeguard themselves and their digital footprint.

🚀

4️⃣ How We Help Users

Through our step-by-step tutorials, interactive quizzes, and real-world case studies, users can learn at their own pace and gain confidence in protecting their online presence. Our certification programs recognize learners' efforts and encourage continued engagement.

Our Vision

To make the internet safer for everyone.

Our Values

Education, empowerment, accessibility, and awareness.
