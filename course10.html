üîπ Immediate Steps:
  1. Do not panic ‚Äî stay calm and document everything
  2. Disconnect affected devices from the internet if hacked
  3. Change passwords for compromised accounts immediately
  4. Save evidence: Screenshots, emails, messages, transaction details

Acting quickly prevents further damage and increases the chance of recovery.
